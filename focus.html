Investment Focus

Strategy

Our focus is on businesses with predictable revenue, manageable complexity, and clear levers for improvement.

We evaluate opportunities based on consistency of earnings, operational simplicity, and the ability to enhance performance without relying on external factors.

While industry-agnostic, we tend to favor models with recurring demand, low customer concentration risk, and straightforward service or digital delivery.

Sectors

• E-commerce
• SaaS
• Blogs
• Real Estate

Typical Investment Profile

• Revenue: $100K – $10M
• Positive or near-positive cash flow
• Simple, understandable business models
• Opportunities to improve margins or scale
