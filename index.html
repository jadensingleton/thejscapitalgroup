About

JS Capital Group is a private investment firm focused on acquiring and operating profitable businesses across the lower middle market. We take a long-term, value-driven approach to building and scaling companies.

Investment Criteria

• Revenue: $100K – $5M
• Strong or improving profitability
• E-commerce, SaaS, and digital businesses
• Owner transition or exit scenarios

Approach

We prioritize efficient execution, flexible deal structures, and long-term ownership. Our goal is to preserve what works while identifying opportunities to increase value through operational improvements and strategic growth.
